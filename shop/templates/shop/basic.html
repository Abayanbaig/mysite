Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [1-Aug-2024]

Thank you for choosing Mizaan Classic for your custom T-shirt printing needs. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website, place orders, or interact with us in any way.

Personal Information: When you place an order or register on our website, we may collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address.

Payment Information: To process orders, we collect payment details such as credit card information. We use secure payment processing services and do not store credit card information on our servers.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our website, such as IP addresses, browser types, pages visited, and timestamps.

2. How We Use Your Information

Order Fulfillment: We use your personal information to process and fulfill your orders, communicate with you about your orders, and provide customer support.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
